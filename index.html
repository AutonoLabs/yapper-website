FAQ

Questions families actually ask.

Will my grandparent know it's AI?
Many people we work with settle into a natural conversation rhythm with Yapper. Families often tell us their parent starts sharing stories from the calls without prompting.
How well does Yapper communicate with older adults?
Yapper is tuned for older-adult conversations. It speaks clearly, leaves space, and follows their pace. If something is unclear, it repeats gently and moves on naturally.
Can Yapper tell me if something is wrong?
Yes. After every call, you get a summary of what was discussed and how your parent seemed. If Yapper detects signs of distress, withdrawal, pain, or cognitive changes, you'll be alerted so you can follow up.
What if they don't want to talk some days?
That's completely fine. Yapper always asks for consent at the start of each call. If they're not in the mood, Yapper says goodbye warmly and tries again next time. No pressure.
Does my parent need any technology?
Not at all. Yapper calls them on their regular phone — landline or mobile. They just answer and talk. No apps, no devices, nothing to learn.
What happens on the calls?
Whatever your parent wants to talk about — memories, family stories, hobbies, current events, or just how their day is going. You can also add context before a call — a birthday, a doctor visit — so Yapper brings it up naturally.
